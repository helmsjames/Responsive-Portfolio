About Me

Hello, my name is James Helms, and I'm a former U.S. Army aviator and federal employee who is now a full-stack web developer with credentials from Southern Methodist University. I gained new skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Mongo database, Express, NodeJS, React, and other technologies. I enjoy using technology to improve business processes and achieved this in my former role by facilitating the creation of an application that saved approximately 25 man-hours per day. I have always sought a better way to do something and using technology helped me accomplish that. My experience as a manager in both civilian and military environments has developed the ability to interact with others and function at various levels. I look forward to continuing my development as a full-stack web developer by creating mobile-first responsive design applications. I am excited to leverage and hone my skills in the future as part of a fast-paced, quality-driven team.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy hiking, camping, and fishing with my teenage daughter. I can usually be found on or near the water trying to catch a monster bass while teaching my daughter about life. Or, just being quiet and learning a lesson or two from her.
